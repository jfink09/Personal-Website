About What I Do

Aerospace Engineer

I graduated with honors in aerospace engineering from The Pennsylvania State University in May 2021. Here, I performed two years of academic research; one year in biomedical engineering and one year in fluid mechanics. I had the opportunity to intern for an aircraft structures company in 2019 and complete the pre-medicine curriculum. I wrapped up my undergraduate career by completing a thesis for my fluid mechanics research and presented it at the AIAA Regional Conference.

Stock Trader

I am a CANSLIM style swing trader with approximately 6 years of experience in the financial markets. I continue to extensively study the methodologies developed by market wizards like William J. O'Neil, Mark Minervini, Nicolas Darvas, Stan Weinstein, and more. My thoughts, analyses, and trading can be followed by keeping up with me on Twitter. I am interested in financial modeling and trading programs that perform time series analysis, backtesting, and machine learing. I plan to eventually test my own system in a small algorithmic trading account to see the extent to which it can be automated.

Builder

I spend a bulk of my time building small projects mostly by website and app development. I am working to expand my skills in the process of seeing my ideas come to fruition. I had built a stock portfolio tracker that focuses on risk management, a goal managing platform that helps users break down large goals into doable tasks, and I am currently working on building a productivity tracker. I also spend some time working on financial models and trading related programs for fun. My goal is to see a product through from start to finish and take it to market. I want to repeat the process and keep working on bigger and bigger projects.
