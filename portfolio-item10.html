WebappDeepFundus

deepfundus.com

I am currently working on building a deep learning platform for retinal disease prediction. Users can upload images of funduscopic images and the convolutional neural network will predict the disease. This is a work in progress as I only have six diseases and normal fundus trained. The diseases I trained are diabetic retinopathy, pathological myopia, retinitis pigmentosa, macular degeneration, central retinal vein occlusion (CRVO), and laser spots which is not a disease. Laser spots form on the retina after someone undergoes retinal laser surgery. Even though this is not a disease, I am training the model to recognize it as a disease so that it can classify it in another way than normal as the appearance is different.

I plan to increase the accuracy of my current model by making each disease dataset larger. I will also try transfer learning with other models. I have tried TinyVGG, EfficientNet B0, and EfficientNet B7. EfficientNet B0 works the best so far. I would like to try ResNet50 next since many published medical projects involving deep learning on medical images also used this model. This is my first project involving deep learning and I am learning a lot about it.
